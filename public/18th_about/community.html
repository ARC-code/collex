Community

Setting up a free Collex account is the easiest way to become a member of the 18thConnect community. Sign up today to access to a personalized MY 18th page, where you can organize your collections, tag and annotate items and share your research using the Exhibit Builder

 

Everyday users of 18thConnect are important contributors to the project. We encourage you to create a Collex account which will let you collect, tag, and annotate digital objects you locate in 18thConnect as part of your own research. The tags you apply to objects in your personal collections then become part of the Collex system, helping other users locate and re-purpose scholarly materials.

18thConnect is always in search of new projects and digital materials to be aggregated into the Collex interface. If you administer a project in nineteenth-century studies that you would like to be peer-reviewed by the 18thConnect Editorial Boards please contact us at $$$$.
