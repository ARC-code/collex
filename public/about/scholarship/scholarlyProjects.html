Scholarly Projects in NINES

NINES has developed an innovative, federated online publishing model and the software system to support it. Listed below are the important 19th-century scholarly projects, journals, and archives that have been peer-reviewed by the Editorial Boards and integrated into the Collex interface. In addition, a number of useful online resources and collections have also been aggregated in order to make NINES a robust searching environment.
