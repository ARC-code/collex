NINES is organized into three content areas, each with its own editorial board. The boards oversee NINES efforts in Americanist, Romantic, and Victorian scholarship.

The steering committee responsible for constituting these boards and driving institutional development of NINES is made up of the following scholars:

  • Stephanie Browner, Berea College
  • John Bryant, Hofstra University
  • Jay Clayton, Vanderbilt University
  • Morris Eaves, University of Rochester
  • Michael Eberle-Sinatra, Université de Montréal
  • Dino Felluga, Purdue University
  • Neil Fraistat, University of Maryland
  • Sharon Harris, University of Connecticut
  • Steve Jones, Loyola University, Chicago
  • Mark Kamrath, University of Central Florida
  • George Landow, Brown University
  • Laura Mandell, Miami University, Ohio
  • Jerome McGann, University of Virginia
  • Joel Myerson, University of South Carolina
  • Kenneth Price, University of Nebraska, Lincoln
  • Martha Nell Smith, University of Maryland
  • Andy Stauffer, Boston University
  • Perry Willett, University of Michigan

In addition, a research and development group under the direction of Jerome McGann at the University of Virginia temporarily serves as NINES headquarters. Contact: technologies@nines.org
