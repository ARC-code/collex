NINES has developed an innovative, federated online publishing model and the software system to support it. We are now working to aggregate a number of important 19th-century scholarly journals, groups, and archives.

A pilot implementation of NINES, released in December 2005, included the Rossetti Archive, the Walt Whitman Archive and Whitman Annotated Bibliography, the Poetess Archive, the Swinburne Project, and the Romantic Circles Praxis Series.

A summer 2006 release of NINES will add Romanticism on the Net, the Victorian Studies Bibliography, the Emily Dickinson Project, the Charles Chesnutt Digital Archive, the Blake Archive, Collective Biographies of Women, the Ambrose Bierce Project and Journal, British Women Romantic Poets, the Whistler Correspondence, and more material from Romantic Circles. Other notable projects will follow, including some brought in through our summer fellowships.

See our listing of associated groups for other NINES-affliated scholarly work.
