Full text search through NINES is currently available for many NINES projects and resources, including materials from JSTOR, Project MUSE and ProQuest. In addition, users may search for titles, names, genres, dates, and other basic information across all participating NINES sites.

In the coming months, we expect to integrate major contributions from Leigh Hunt Online: The Letters, and Ohio State University Press, as well as updated and expanded information from the Dickinson Project, Romantic Circles, The Poetess Archive and Romanticism and Victorianism on the Net.

See our listing of associated groups for other NINES-affiliated scholarly work.
