The current situation: NINES is presently in planning and building phases, which involve consultation with interested scholars and institutions and the construction of a suite of tools to support digital publication, collaboration, and analysis. A pilot implementation of NINES was released in December 2005 and is being radically expanded in Summer 2006. Some 40,0000 digital objects are now integrated for faceted browsing and user annotation through Collex.

Try out the NINES/Collex system online now!

The NINES editorial boards are currently compiling guidelines for vettors and contributors. Although we are not yet actively vetting contributions to NINES, we are happy to consult with and offer advice to potential contributors, who may also be interested in applying for future summer workshops in digitization.
