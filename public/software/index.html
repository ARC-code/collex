NINES Software Development

The development of these standards-compliant, open-source technologies began under the auspices of Applied Research in Patacriticism (ARP) and the Speculative Computing Laboratory (SpecLab), its research affiliate at the University of Virginia. All of these tools are available for download or online use. More information about each can be found by clicking the corresponding links.

COLLEX

Collex is the collecting/exhibiting tool that powers NINES. Visit the Collex page and the Collex blog to learn more.

JUXTA

Juxta is a textual collation tool for scholars. Visit the Juxta page and the Juxta blog to download the newest version.

IVANHOE

Ivanhoe is an interactive playspace for exploring textuality. Visit the IVANHOE page and the Ivanhoe blog to start a new game.
